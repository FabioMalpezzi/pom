# Wiki Log

Append-only chronological register of wiki updates.

Recommended format:

```text
## [YYYY-MM-DD] action | detail
```

Typical actions:

- `ingest`
- `query`
- `update`
- `lint`
- `reconciliation`
- `promotion`

## [YYYY-MM-DD] init | wiki creation

- Source: project initialization.
- Changes: created wiki index/log and first wiki pages.
- Linked decisions: none.
- Verification: none.
